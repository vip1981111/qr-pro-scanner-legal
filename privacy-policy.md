# Privacy Policy for QR Pro Scanner

**Last updated:** January 16, 2026

## Information We Collect

QR Pro Scanner does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Camera Permission

The app requires camera access to scan QR codes. All scanning is done locally on your device. We do not store or transmit any images.

## Photo Library Permission

The app requires photo library access only to save generated QR codes to your device. We do not access, upload, or share your photos.

## Data Storage

- All scan history is stored locally on your device
- - Data is never transmitted to external servers
  - - You can delete your scan history at any time from the app
